The Giving Tree

We are a university-based organization that collects used clothes for donation. Our mission is to help those in need and promote sustainability through the reuse of clothing.

Donate Clothes

If you would like to donate clothes, please contact us at donate@givingtree.org. We accept gently used clothing items and accessories such as shoes, hats, and purses. We appreciate your support!
